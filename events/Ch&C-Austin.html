March 10, 2026 • 5 – 8 PM • Seven Grand, Austin

Join CockroachDB for an exclusive evening where resilient distributed systems meet exceptional single malt whiskey.

Cheers & Clusters Austin: Survivability & Single Malts is designed for engineers, architects, and technical leaders who build and operate mission-critical applications. We'll explore how modern organizations design databases that survive—from regional outages to unpredictable traffic—followed by a guided whiskey tasting in a relaxed, social setting.

Agenda – What we'll cover:

– Real-world CockroachDB use cases
How teams support demanding workloads like Identity & Access Management and Payments with strong consistency, fault tolerance, and horizontal scale.

– Key SQL differentiators for mission-critical systems
Why distributed SQL matters for correctness, performance, and operational simplicity at scale.

– Building AI-ready applications with CockroachDB
Architectures and patterns for supporting modern AI and data-intensive workloads.

– Live demo: Vector search with CockroachDB
See how vector capabilities integrate directly into the database—no fragile pipelines required.

After the technical session, unwind with a curated guided whiskey tasting, featuring select single malts and plenty of time for conversation with peers and CockroachDB. We hope to see you there.

This event is exclusively intended for relevant Cockroach Labs customers, partners, and prospects. We reserve the right to decline registrations that do not fall within these categories.
