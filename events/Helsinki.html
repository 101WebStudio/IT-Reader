April 15, 2026 • 15:00 – 18:30 • NH Collection Helsinki Grand Hansa, Helsinki

On April 15, Keepit Live Nordic Roadshow comes to Helsinki with an intimate executive event featuring Jarno Limnéll, Finnish member of parliament and one of the country's leading experts on cybersecurity and security policy, alongside Keepit's CISO Kim Larsen.

Together, they will explore how geopolitical tensions, cyberthreats, and regulation increasingly shape the security decisions organizations must make — and what leaders should be aware of as responsibility for data, compliance, and resilience continues to grow.

Keepit Live is not about products. It's a forum for knowledge sharing, perspective, and dialogue — where executive peers come together to gain insight into how others approach today's most pressing security challenges.

Program:

15:00: Welcome and introduction

15:15–17:00: Speaker session and roundtable discussion: Geopolitics, cybersecurity, and digital resilience

17:00–18:30: Networking and light refreshments

About the speakers:

Jarno Limnéll is a Finnish member of parliament, professor of practice in cybersecurity, and one of Finland's most recognized voices on cybersecurity and societal resilience. His experience spans academia, policy, and international security cooperation. Jarno is known for shaping the national debate on cyberthreats, information warfare, and hybrid influence and for bringing a strong policy- and board-level perspective to digital security.

Kim Larsen is CISO at Keepit with more than two decades of experience in cybersecurity, risk, and compliance. Previously CISO at the Danish Tax Agency (SKAT), Systematic, and Huawei, he has worked in some of Europe's most complex and highly regulated environments. Kim advises executive leadership on how geopolitics, regulation, and cyber risk translate into concrete decisions around data protection and resilience.

This event is exclusively intended for IT professionals and data protection decision-makers. We reserve the right to decline registrations that do not fall within these categories.
